Ingredients:


  • 2 eggs
  • Any type of oil (Avacado oil is recomended)
  • A pan
  • Salt

Here's how to make eggs:


  1. Put the pan on the stove and turn on the heat.
  2. Pour some oil onto the pan and tilt the pan
    so the oil covers the surface.
  3. Crack the egg open on the edge of the pan
    and pour the yolk into the pan.
    Make sure you don't let the egg shell fall into the pan!
  4. Do the same with the second egg.
    Be sure to wash your hands afterwards!
  5. Let the eggs cook for about a minute,
    then flip the eggs over with your spatula.
  6. Flip the eggs as many times as required
    and let them cook until they stop looking wet.
  7. Once the eggs look done, use the spatula to pick them up and put them onto a plate.
  8. Now your eggs are finished cooking!
    You can put some salt on top of them to enhance the flavor,
    or you can experiment with other spices.
