This is a guide to making toast


Toast is a very delicious side that
pairs well with eggs and sausage.


Ingredients:


  • Loaf of bread
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Toaster
  • Optional:
    • Oregano
    • Garlic

Instructions:


  1. Get two slices of bread from the loaf.
  2. Put both slices into the toaster.
  3. This step requires experience with different types of bread.
    Some bread takes less time to cook, and some bread
    takes a very long time to cook.
    Experiment with different temperatures until you find the right one.
    Try to toast the bread to the point where it's golden brown.
  4. Once the bread is toasted, you have two options:
    spray the bread with butter spray,
    or spread real butter on top of the toast.
    Spreading the butter takes more time and experience,
    while spraying the butter is extremely unhealthy.
  5. After you spread the butter, you should put some salt on top.
  6. For more flavor, put some oregano and garlic on top.
  7. Now, you can enjoy your toast!
