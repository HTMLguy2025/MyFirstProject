Adding more flavor to your eggs


Sometimes, just adding salt to your eggs can taste a little plain.
This page will show you some ideas for adding flavor to your eggs.


  1. Spicy eggs

    If you like spicy food, then you can
    add garlic powder, a little cayenne pepper,
    and some oregano.

  2. Flavorful eggs

    This spice blend is different than most,
    but it can taste really good.
    Try adding celery salt and curry.
    It works much better with scrambled eggs,
    so try not to use it on fried eggs.

  3. The classic

    If you want to have more flavor than just salt,
    but you also want something simple,
    then just add salt, garlic, pepper, and oregano.
